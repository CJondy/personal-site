Website Evaluations (Disliked)

Website One

Yale University School of Art

Evaluation:

The website is very confusing to look at. The background is repeated over and over, do to
the fact the image is not correctly sized to be a background image. The colors are also a
bit hard to look at some being very bright while others being darker. It is very hard to
navigate through the website and find what you are looking for exactly.

 

Website Two

The Room Movie

Evaluation:

The website itself is very informative I'll give them that but the repetition of words is quite
annoying. The sizes of the words all different from each other so it is very hard for the
website viewer to actually read the contents of the website. The structure of the website is
also annoying to look at and hard to find what you are looking for. 

 

Website Three

PAWS

Evaluation:

The school's student website is pretty outdated compared to other colleges' student
portals. It isn't the most pleasing website to look at and the text could be bigger for those
not able to see well. Finding what you are looking for also isn't very hard, but it could be
much easier.
